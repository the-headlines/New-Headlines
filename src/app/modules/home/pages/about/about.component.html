• About

    Every day we consume a great deal of news, much of which may not be particularly relevant or important to us.

    We have developed a platform which creates awareness and can have a positive impact on our way of thinking. After all the betterment of human life is the sole purpose of any idea.

    NOT THE HEADLINES is an social news website where users post news links and readers decide upon the headlines by voting for their favourites.

    A score, based on votes, views, questions and comments, is given to each post. The post with highest score will make it into website's headline

    News which has not featured prominently in the mainstream media can become the headline on the NOT THE HEADLINES website.
